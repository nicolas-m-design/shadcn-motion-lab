# Article Notes

## Working angle

What changed in my interaction design and frontend workflow once AI became part of the loop?

## Throughline

- AI changed the speed and shape of interaction exploration, not just code generation
- motion became easier to test when paired with clearer state and intent
- `shadcn/ui` was useful as scaffolding because it removed low-value setup work
- lightweight specs were enough when the demos and states stayed concrete
- the biggest gains came from faster iteration, not from handing over full authorship
